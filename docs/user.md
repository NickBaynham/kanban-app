# Using the Kanban board

This app is a single project board with **five columns**. You can rename columns, add and delete cards, and move cards by dragging. Nothing is stored on a server: if you refresh the page, the board resets to the built-in sample data.

## Columns

Each column has a title at the top. **Click the title** to edit it, then press Enter or click away to apply. There are always exactly five columns.

## Cards

Each card has a **title** and **details**. Use **+ Add card** at the bottom of a column, fill in the fields, and choose **Add card** (the purple button). **Delete** removes a card from the board.

## Moving cards

Use the **grip** control on the left of a card to **drag** it. You can reorder cards within a column or drop a card into another column.

## What is not included

There is no login, no saving to disk or cloud, no search, and no archive. The goal is a simple, focused board.
